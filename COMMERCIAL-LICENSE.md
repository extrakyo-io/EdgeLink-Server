## Why dual licensing?

GPL-3.0 protects the open source nature of this project: anyone who improves it must share their improvements back to the community. This is great for the open ecosystem, but conflicts with closed-source product development.

The commercial license lets you integrate EdgeLink Server / SDK into proprietary software **without** the GPL "copyleft" requirements — your code stays closed, you keep ownership, your product can be sold under your own terms.

This is the same model used by MySQL, Qt, Redis, GitLab, and many other dual-licensed projects.
